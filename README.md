# XFF benchmark
Input structures and results for XFF benchmark

Created by Bai Xue and Qiaochu Zhang, XtalPi Inc., Shenzhen, China.

May 2023

## Manuscript 
Bai et al. "Development and Comprehensive Benchmark of a High Quality AMBER-Consistent Small Molecule Force Field with Broad Chemical Space Coverage for Molecular Modeling
and Free Energy Calculation"

## Data
**fep:** input structures and results for FEP simulations

**conformer\_validation:** list of molecule IDs from the public OpenFF Industrial benchmark data set used in the manuscript, their corresponding XFF results
